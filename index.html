Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quiz LM. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Information from Google: When you sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Quiz Data: We track your quiz attempts, scores, and progress within the app to provide you with your performance history.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate and identify you as a user.
  • To save your quiz progress and performance.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our application.

4. Your Rights

Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we process.
  • Correct or erase your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account.

5. Data Security

We use secure services provided by Supabase to store and protect your data. All data is handled with strict confidentiality.

6. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under the age of 18. We require all users to confirm they are 18 or older before creating an account.

7. Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings panel within the app. This action is irreversible.

User Guide

Build a Custom Quiz

Navigate to the "Build a Custom Quiz" section from the homepage. Use the extensive filters for subject, topic, difficulty, and more to create a quiz tailored to your needs. Your progress is saved if you need to leave and come back.

Create Study Materials

Use the same filters to select questions and then choose to generate a bilingual PowerPoint (PPT), an English-only PDF, or a JSON file for your own projects. It's perfect for offline study and presentations.

Review & Analyze

After completing a quiz, you get a detailed score report. Use the "Review Answers" feature to go through each question, check the correct answers, and read detailed explanations to understand the concepts better.

Personalize Your Experience

Open the settings menu to toggle dark mode, sound effects, question shuffling, and background animations to make the app experience truly your own.

Content Creation Tools

Select your desired format to begin. You will be taken to the filter page to select the questions for your document.
